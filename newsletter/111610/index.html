On Call:
Meet Christopher L. Belleau, M.D.

Dr. Christopher Belleau graduated from LSU Medical School, in 1990, and completed a Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation residency at Charity Hospital in New Orleans, in 1994. He is Board certified by the American Board of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation. He serves as Associate Medical Director of the Rehabilitation Center at OLOL RMC and Medical Director of SAGE Rehab Hospital. Dr. Belleau coordinates rehabilitation for post operative patients. He also performs EMG/nerve conduction studies and interventional spine procedures including epidural steroid injections and related procedures. Other interests include musculoskeletal ultrasound and chronic wound care.

Did You Know:
Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery
- Kevin P. McCarthy , M.D.

The Spine Center at Bone & Joint Clinic focuses on a comprehensive approach to the treatment of spinal disorders. Treatment is individualized to each patient’s symptoms and pathology. A variety of treatment options are available including advanced physical therapy, medical treatments, pain management injections, and minimally invasive spine procedures. Minimally invasive spine surgeries available to appropriate patients include endoscopic diskectemy, endoscopic radiofrequency rhizotomies, percutaneous fusion procedures, and a variety of minimally invasive decompression and spinal fusion procedures.

The Spine Center is led by Dr. Kevin McCarthy, a board certified and fellowship trained specialist in adult and pediatric spine surgery. The facility has available resources for physical therapy, radiographs, MRI, neuro-diagnostic studies, and an outpatient surgical center for performance of pain management and outpatient spinal procedures. Learn more at bjcbr.com/spine
