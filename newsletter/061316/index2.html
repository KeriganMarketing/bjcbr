Sporting Goods:
Top Tips for a Safe Summer

Summer tends to be an active time of year when with outdoor activities and adventures. Have fun and keep it safe with these tips:

1. Wear a helmet: Whether biking, skateboarding, roller skating or riding a scooter, helmets should be worn at all times. According to safekids.org, properly-fitted helmets can reduce the risk of head injuries by at least 45%.

2. Use a life jacket: Boating and other water activities like water skiing and tubing are popular summertime activities. Wearing a Coast Guard approved life jacket is a critical component of these activities that keeps everyone safe and allows everyone to enjoy some fun in the sun... Read more.

Did You Know:
Laser Spine Surgery Now Available

Minimally Invasive and Laser Spine Surgery is now available in Baton Rouge! Dr. Kevin McCarthy and Dr. Chambliss Harrod are proud to offer laser technology to relieve back pain and other spine conditions through a partnership with The Spine Hospital of Louisiana.

Minimally invasive spine surgery allows a surgeon to accomplish similar goals as open spine surgery but through a much smaller incision with less tissue disruption. The result is pain relief and return of function with less blood loss, shorter hospital stay, quicker return to activities, and less need for pain medicine... Read more.
